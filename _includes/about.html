Welcome to Quantified Golf! My name is Will Wagner, a long time resident of the San Franscisco Bay Area for which I've called home for over 30 years. I've been very fortunate to have a career in software development and managing teams and products. Along with work, family, and travel, golf is a passon of mine.

I started golfing when I was a kid, and much of my teenage years were spent either working, practicing, or playing golf. My parents were golfer too, and I have many fond memories playing skins with my dad and his friends

I've taken long hiatus both to raise a family and focus on my careeer, but now in my mid-50's, the passion for the sport has been reignited

Quantified Golf is a resource for the game of golf outside of the golf swing. Curiosity and an engineering mindset helped me to contribute to great software products and companies, and I believe those tools can also help you get better at golf

You won't find golf swing tips here, at least nothing more than a link to people far better at understanding the golf swing. However, I think taking a data driven approach to golf can help you make better decisions both on the course, as well as you can practice most effectively.

I hope you find this site helpful, and even if you don't agree with all of it, I hope it at least gives you fuel to reassess your own game and practice strategies

I wish you good golf and great memories!
